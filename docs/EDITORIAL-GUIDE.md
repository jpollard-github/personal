# EDITORIAL-GUIDE.md

Use this alongside `docs/CONTENT-TODO.md`.

Concrete publishing tasks belong in `docs/CONTENT-TODO.md`.

Responsibility:

This document owns voice, tone, and publishing philosophy rather than the task backlog.

## How These Work Together

- `CONTENT-TODO.md` tracks what to publish next.
- `EDITORIAL-GUIDE.md` keeps the voice, tone, and publishing philosophy consistent.
- `low-friction-content-flow.md` explains how raw captured material becomes a post, draft, or update.

## ArcadeGhosts Voice

Keep the writing personal, specific, warm, and a little strange.

The site should sound like Jason, not like polished brand filler.

## Tiny Thoughts

Tiny Thoughts keep the site alive.

Publish them often, keep them light, and do not wait for perfection.

## Essays

Essays explain meaning.

Use them for reflection, memory, taste, identity, and the deeper reasons something matters.

## Build Log

Build Log explains progress.

Use it to show active making, decisions, small lessons, false starts, and momentum.

## Music Commentary

Music writing should explain why a song, record, or mood matters, not just list favorites.

## Books

Book writing should feel curated and personal, not encyclopedic.

Start small and explain why each book belongs.

## Projects

Project writing should connect the work to story, intent, and what was learned.

## Future Rooms

New rooms should emerge from real content energy, not from a desire to add structure.

## Publishing Philosophy

- Publish often.
- Keep personality visible.
- Do not wait for perfection.
- Let Build Log show progress.
- Let essays carry meaning.
- Let Tiny Thoughts keep the site alive.
- Improve existing rooms before multiplying new ones.
