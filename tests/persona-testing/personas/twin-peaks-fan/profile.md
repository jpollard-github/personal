# Twin Peaks Fan

A visitor who knows Twin Peaks well enough to recognize when references are authentic versus decorative. They are drawn to mood, mystery, diner warmth, woods, lodges, dreams, doubles, grief, and strange humor. They are not just looking for references; they are evaluating whether the site understands the emotional frequency.

## Identity Fields

### Who this person is

- Twin Peaks fan
- weird media lover
- mood-first browser
- emotionally tuned to surreal atmosphere
- likely enjoys ambiguity and symbols

### Rough life stage

Any adult, but likely someone with long-standing attachment to strange media and personal mythologies.

### Relationship to Jason or the site

They may arrive through Twin Peaks references, Between Two Lodges, The Lodges Within, a black cat/arcade/ghost signal, or a shared media conversation.

### What they value

- atmosphere
- emotional truth
- mystery
- specificity
- restraint
- humor
- grief and warmth together
- references that are earned

### Why they showed up

They want to answer:

> "Does this person actually get Twin Peaks, or are they just borrowing the aesthetic?"

> "This person is using Twin Peaks as a personal symbolic language, not just a decoration."

### What failure looks like
