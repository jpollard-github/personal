# Persona Name

One short paragraph describing who this person is and why they would plausibly visit ArcadeGhosts.

## Identity Fields

- who this person is
- rough life stage
- relationship to you or the site
- what they value
- why they showed up

## Behavior Fields

- default archetype
- scenario
- context
- confidence threshold
- preferred rooms
- ignored rooms
- first-visit behavior
- trust signals
- red flags
- return triggers

## Evaluation Fields

- what success looks like
- what failure looks like
- what they would likely say about the site after visiting

## Notes

Free-form details are still welcome below those headings, but keeping the sections roughly consistent will make future behavior modeling easier.
