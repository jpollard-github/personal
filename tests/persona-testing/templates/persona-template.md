# Persona Name

One short paragraph describing who this person is and why they would plausibly visit ArcadeGhosts.

## Who this person is

- age range
- rough life stage
- relationship to you or the site
- why they showed up

## Personality

Describe the person's curiosity, social energy, warmth, skepticism, patience, and openness.

## Emotional style

Describe whether they want clarity, reassurance, excitement, novelty, calm, playfulness, or depth.

## Work / curiosity

Describe how technical or non-technical they are, how much they enjoy learning, and whether they enjoy software, design, writing, systems, or ideas.

## Interests

List the themes or rooms they are likely to care about.

Examples:

- software projects
- writing
- music
- movies
- cats
- local life
- AI tools
- weird internet rooms

## Lifestyle

Describe their pace and habits.

Examples:

- coffee shops
- bookstores
- long walks
- travel
- family life
- late-night browsing

## Conversation starters

What would make them want to keep clicking?

Examples:

- personal story
- reflective writing
- practical project
- favorite albums
- cat stories
- local recommendations

## Deal breakers

What makes them bounce?

Examples:

- too cluttered
- too self-promotional
- too cold
- too confusing
- too much internal/admin energy leaking into public pages

## Visual or usability preferences

Describe how they react to:

- dense homepages
- bright color
- dark interfaces
- long copy
- scrolling
- too many choices
- playful weirdness

## What they hope to find

List the top 3-5 outcomes they want from the site visit.

## What would delight them

Describe the kinds of details that would feel especially "for them."
